Income: Calendar-Based Tracking

The calendar shows planned and actual income totals per day. Tap a day to see entries and weekly totals.

Planned Income vs Actual Income

If your paycheck is consistent, create recurring actual income. If it varies, use planned income to estimate and log actual income when it arrives.

How Income Feeds the App

Income entries contribute to Home and Budget calculations. Actual income drives real totals and savings, while planned income supports forecasts.
