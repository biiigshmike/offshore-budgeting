# Presets

## Presets: Reusable Fixed Expense Templates
[[screenshot:1]]
Use presets for fixed bills like rent or subscriptions. Tap + to create one. Swipe right to edit or left to delete.

## How Presets Affect Totals
[[screenshot:2]]
When assigned to a budget, presets become planned expenses in that budget.
- Presets act as templates for planned expenses.
- Planned expenses generated from presets use planned amount unless you edit actual amount later.

## Tip
[[screenshot:3]]
Use presets to make budget setup fast and consistent month to month.
